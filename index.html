Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Freight Flow platform.

How does load matching work? ▼

Our AI-powered engine automatically analyzes your cargo requirements and matches them with available carriers in real-time, ensuring optimal route efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

How are carriers verified? ▼

We conduct a multi-step background check, including license validation, insurance verification, and historical performance reviews for every carrier in our network.

Can I track shipments in real time? ▼

Yes! Our platform provides GPS-based tracking, allowing you and your customers to monitor the exact location and status of your shipments at any time.

How do payments work? ▼

We utilize a secure, encrypted digital payment gateway that automates invoicing upon proof-of-delivery, ensuring carriers are paid faster and shippers have clear audit trails.

Is there a mobile app? ▼

Yes, VEXA TECH is available on both iOS and Android. Drivers can use the app for real-time tracking, digital document uploads, and instant load notifications.
